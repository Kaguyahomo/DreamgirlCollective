I Saw Her Standing There

Catchy! To call this song youthful would be an understatement. What Lennon and McCartney call love I’d wager is just in their ragers. The hand claps as percussion goes a long way in creating the atmosphere of this being performed at a live event, where the scene of the song would occur. Likewise since this first album of theirs is all the catalog they had from doing live shows, it was probably a scene the band had seen many times. The song certainly isn’t sonically complex, but leading off their debut with an original instead of the covers is pretty savvy!.
